# Privacy Policy for Fresh Keeper

**Effective Date:** January 13, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mobile Craft ("we," "us," or "our"), operating from India, collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Fresh Keeper mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Fresh Keeper, we collect:

- **Email Address:** Used for account creation, authentication, customer support, and sending important notifications about your account
- **Google Account Information:** When you sign in using Google OAuth, we receive your email address and basic profile information (name, profile picture) from Google
- **Device Token:** Used to send push notifications to your device about product expiry reminders and referral updates
- **Referral Code:** If you share your referral code with others or use someone else's code

### 1.2 Information You Create in the App

- **Product Inventory Data:** Items you add to track, including product names, expiry dates, purchase dates, prices (optional), quantities, and categories
- **Shopping Lists:** Items you add to your shopping lists (Premium feature)
- **Usage Data:** Information about whether products were used or marked as wastage (Premium feature)
- **Wastage Reports:** Analytics generated from your usage patterns (Premium feature)

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Pages or screens visited within the App, time and date of visits, time spent on pages, app crashes, and performance data
- **IP Address:** Your device's Internet Protocol address
- **Analytics Data:** App interactions, feature usage, and performance metrics collected through Firebase Analytics

### 1.4 Information We Do Not Collect

- **Precise Location Data:** We do not collect your GPS location or precise geographic location
- **Note:** AdMob (our advertising partner) may collect approximate location data for ad serving purposes. Please see Section 4 for details about third-party data collection.

### 2.4 Communication

### 2.5 App Improvement
- Analyzing app usage patterns to improve features and user experience
- Identifying and fixing bugs and crashes
- Developing new features based on user behavior

### 2.6 Advertising
- Displaying advertisements through Google AdMob
- Supporting the free version of our App

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is securely stored on servers provided by Supabase, a trusted cloud database service. We also use Firebase services (owned by Google) for:
- Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
- Analytics (Firebase Analytics)
- Crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)
- Cloud Functions for backend processing

### 3.2 Security Measures

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

- Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
- Secure authentication using industry-standard OAuth 2.0
- Regular security assessments of our systems
- Access controls limiting who can access user data
- Secure cloud infrastructure with Supabase and Firebase

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

### 6.1 Access and Update
- You can access and update your account information and inventory data directly within the App at any time

### 6.2 Delete Your Account
- You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at freshkeeperhelp@gmail.com
- Upon receiving your deletion request, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law

### 6.3 Export Your Data
- Premium users can export their inventory and wastage data directly from the App
- You can also request a copy of your data by emailing freshkeeperhelp@gmail.com

### 6.4 Opt-Out of Push Notifications
- You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings or within the App settings
- Note that disabling notifications may affect your ability to receive timely expiry reminders

### 6.5 Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising
- You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings:
  - **Android:** Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  - **iOS:** Go to Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
- You can also reset your advertising ID to limit ad tracking

### 6.6 Delete the App
- You can stop all data collection by uninstalling Fresh Keeper using the standard uninstall process on your device
- Note that uninstalling the App does not automatically delete your account data from our servers. Please email us to request account deletion.
