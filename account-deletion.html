Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go! If you wish to delete your Fresh Keeper account and all associated data, we're here to help you through the process.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all your data, including your product inventory, shopping lists, wastage reports, and any premium subscription benefits.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed from our servers:

Data to be Deleted:

  • Your account information (email, name, profile)
  • All product inventory data (items, expiry dates, prices)
  • Shopping lists and preferences
  • Wastage tracking data and reports
  • Usage analytics and app activity
  • Premium subscription details
  • Referral history and rewards
  • Any notes or custom data you've added

How to Request Account Deletion

Follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at freshkeeperhelp@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • The email address associated with your Fresh Keeper account
    • Confirm that you want to permanently delete your account
    • Optional: Let us know why you're leaving (helps us improve)
  4. We will process your request within 30 days
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Your account and data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your request. During this time, your account will be deactivated and you won't be able to access it. If you change your mind within this period, contact us immediately at freshkeeperhelp@gmail.com to cancel the deletion request.

What About My Subscription?

If you have an active Premium subscription:

  • Your subscription will be cancelled immediately upon account deletion
  • No refunds will be provided for any unused subscription time
  • If you have a subscription through Razorpay, you may need to cancel it separately through your payment provider

Before You Go...

Consider these alternatives to account deletion:

  • Export your data: Use the Export feature (Premium) to save your inventory and wastage history before deleting
  • Take a break: Simply uninstall the app - your data will remain safe if you want to return later
  • Cancel subscription: You can cancel Premium without deleting your account
  • Contact support: If you're facing issues, we're here to help at freshkeeperhelp@gmail.com
